Prickly Pear Coffee Co. Has Opened in Franklin

Franklin's independent coffee scene has a new entry. Prickly Pear Coffee Co. has opened a location in Franklin, adding to a local coffee landscape that has grown steadily alongside the city's broader restaurant and retail expansion.

The Williamson Herald noted the opening as part of a string of new local businesses establishing themselves in Franklin — a pattern that reflects both the city's population growth and the spending power of its resident base.

## Franklin's Coffee Culture

For a city of Franklin's size, the independent coffee scene punches above its weight. Honest Coffee Roasters — the local roaster with multiple Franklin locations — set a high bar for what specialty coffee in Williamson County could look like, and subsequent entrants have generally followed that standard.

Prickly Pear joins that conversation as a new option worth exploring for residents building their regular rotation. Franklin's coffee culture has become a consistent positive mention among relocating families from cities with strong independent coffee scenes — a detail that matters more to some buyers than others but tends to signal broader quality-of-life alignment.

## For New Franklin Residents

If you're new to Franklin and building your list of regular spots, the coffee shop you find yourself returning to weekly will do more to make the city feel like home than almost any other single discovery. Franklin's independent coffee options — Honest, Frothy Monkey, and now Prickly Pear among them — give you real choices.
